# Scope & Goals

Target: detect deepfake-driven crypto airdrops and giveaway scams across social media, video platforms, messaging apps, and on-chain announcements.

Primary goals:
- High precision for flagged items to avoid false takedowns.
- Fast triage and evidence presentation for manual review.
- Wallet-level warnings to prevent user losses.
- Scalable pipeline for ingesting high-volume content.

Constraints:
- Respect platform Terms of Service and privacy laws.
- Minimize PII collection and retention.
